What You're Getting

Your AI assistant is a 24/7 autonomous agent that proactively handles tasks on your behalf. Unlike ChatGPT, which waits for you to ask questions, your agent:

  • Monitors your email and calendar automatically
  • Triages messages and drafts responses
  • Executes workflows while you sleep
  • Learns your preferences over time
  • Connects to your favorite tools (Telegram, Discord, WhatsApp)

Before Your Session

Preparation Checklist

  • Know which email account you want connected
  • Have your calendar app ready (Google Calendar, Outlook, etc.)
  • Decide on your messaging platform (Telegram, Discord, or WhatsApp)
  • Think about 2-3 workflows you want automated
  • Prepare any API keys or login credentials you want integrated

What Your Agent Can Do

With 5,494+ community skills available, your agent can handle almost anything. Here are common use cases:

  • Email Management — Triage inbox, draft responses, flag important messages
  • Calendar — Schedule meetings, send reminders, manage conflicts
  • Research — Monitor news, summarize articles, track competitors
  • Social Media — Post content, engage followers, track mentions
  • Documents — Draft content, organize files, create summaries
  • Automation — Connect tools via Zapier (8,000+ apps), run scheduled tasks

Security & Privacy

Your data stays on your server. We use:

  • OAuth authentication — Your agent never sees raw passwords
  • Docker sandboxing — Isolated execution environment
  • Firewall hardening — Protected from external access
  • Read-only by default — Agent asks before destructive actions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to be technical?

No. After setup, you talk to your AI through Telegram, Discord, or WhatsApp — just like texting an assistant.

What if I already have OpenClaw installed?

We can audit your existing setup, add security hardening, and migrate you to managed care.

How do I add new skills?

Your agent comes with 3 custom workflows. Additional skills can be added anytime: npx clawhub@latest install <skill-name>

What's the difference between Remote and In-Person?

Remote is fully remote deployment via video call. In-Person includes hands-on training at your location (Philly tri-state) and iMessage integration.
