Questions?

What is OpenClaw?

An open-source AI assistant that runs 24/7. It triages email, schedules meetings, drafts replies, and automates workflows — proactively, without you asking.

Do I need to be technical?

Not at all. We handle everything. After setup, you talk to your AI through Telegram, Discord, or WhatsApp just like texting an assistant.

Is it secure?

Yes. We use OAuth middleware so your AI never sees raw credentials. Docker sandboxing, firewall hardening, and read-only permissions by default.

Remote vs In-Person?

Remote is fully remote deployment. In-Person is for Philly tri-state clients who want us on-site with hands-on training and iMessage integration.
