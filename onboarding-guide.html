Security & Privacy

Your security is non-negotiable. Here's how we protect you:

  • OAuth Integration: Your agent never sees raw passwords. We use OAuth to connect your accounts securely.
  • Docker Sandbox: Your agent runs in an isolated container, separated from your main system.
  • Firewall Hardening: Only necessary ports are open. Everything else is locked down.
  • Read-Only by Default: Your agent starts with minimal permissions. We expand access only as you build trust.
  • Your Data, Your Server: Everything runs on your infrastructure. We don't store your data.
